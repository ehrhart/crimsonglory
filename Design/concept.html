Crimson Glory

by

Michal Marcinkowski


Game Concept


Introduction:
Crimson Glory is a multiplayer dogfighting game set in the Mediterranean in an alternative reality after World War I. Combat is team-based, taking the best from games like Triplane Turmoil, The Trenches, Battlefield 1942 and Soldat adding 3D graphics combined with realistic physics. The game world is heavily inspired by Hayao Miyazaki's movie Porco Rosso.

Background:
Crimson Glory is built upon the Crimson Engine also developed by Michal Marcinkowski creator of Soldat. Both game and engine expand the possibilities, gameplay features and development experience from Soldat.

Description:
You take a role in a conflict between the Air Force and the Air Pirates. Both teams struggle to dominate over the Mediterranean. You can select to be either a soldier and participate in ground battle including base defense and assault or be a hydroplane fighter pilot and become the ace of the skies.

Key features:
- unique blend of game types
- dogfighting combined with soldier close-combat and strategy planning
- dedicated multiplayer gaming
- realistic and fun physics including destructible airplanes
- fully modifiable game engine with SDK

Genre:
2D multiplayer WWI dogfighting

Platform:
Primary platform: Windows PC, also targeted at Linux, MacOS and XBox. The game will be playable over the Internet and LAN.
