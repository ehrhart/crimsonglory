Project name:
Crimson Glory

Brief description:
Crimson Glory is a multiplayer air dogfighting game set in the Mediterranean in an alternative reality after World War I. Gameplay will be similar to Triplane Turmoil and Soldat but with 3D graphics. The game world is heavily inspired by Hayao Miyazaki's movie "Porco Rosso" (manga sample).

Developed by:
Michal Marcinkowski

Team members needed:

1) Creative map makers
Maps can be created in any 3D modelling tool.
The final map is done in DeleD

Team members should be responsible, creative and willing to follow guidelines (in that order). Individuals that not only want to deliver content for the game but take part in creating and designing the game are most welcome.

Technology:
Custom developed Crimson Engine. Capable of loading all popular 3D models and texture formats. format.
